About Me

Hi! I'm Alyza Anzano a professional baker who loves crafting pastries, breads, and desserts made with love and creativity. Baking is both an art and a science that involves preparing food using dry heat, typically in an oven. It transforms simple ingredients like flour, sugar, eggs, and butter into delicious creations such as cakes, bread, cookies, and pastries.
